Rhyll — Phillip Island

Rebuilt From the Storm

Residential Renovation & Extension

After a storm caused severe structural damage, we rebuilt from near-scratch — connecting a new build seamlessly to the existing tower and bungalow that survived. Five bedrooms. Three bathrooms. A central deck where everyone meets. The view to the water is unobstructed. There is nothing in front of it. This one was personal.

A Place for Men to Heal

Commercial Conversion — Design by Lauren McKillop

A terrace brick home became a fully soundproofed psychological practice — designed specifically as a space for men to feel safe enough to do the work. No expense was spared on warmth, function, and acoustic integrity. Honed marble at the front desk. Microcement bathrooms. Every room designed to welcome, not intimidate. Lauren McKillop's design language found the perfect builder in Templ.
