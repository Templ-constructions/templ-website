Built From the Ground Up.

I've been in the building industry since 2010. Not because someone told me to — because I couldn't imagine doing anything else. From the first time I picked up a tool, I found something I didn't just love, but something I was built for.

Over sixteen years I've worked alongside some of Melbourne's most respected builders, learning a standard of architectural craftsmanship that most people never encounter on a residential project. That education was deliberate. I knew early that when I built something under my own name, it would have to be different.

I started Templ during COVID. While others waited, I was studying, registering, and quietly building a client base that believed in the same things I did — that a home built with genuine care looks and feels completely different to one that wasn't.

Templ isn't just a building company. It's my standard on every site, every day.

Who We Build For

Our clients don't always start out certain. They're discerning people — often business owners, often high-achievers — who have been let down by the industry before or simply know enough to be careful.

They're not looking for the cheapest quote. They're looking for someone who will understand their brief deeply enough to improve it. Someone whose judgment they can trust on site, whose communication doesn't leave them guessing, and whose finished product they'll still be proud of in twenty years.

If that sounds like you, we should talk.
